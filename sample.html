The Colonial Heritage Festival is the largest American colonial living and reenactment event in the western United States. Historic interpreters set up an 18th century village complete with a cooper, blacksmith, broom maker, printer, baker, potter, basket maker, gunsmith, hatter, silversmith, spoon maker, chandler, cannon, and both continental and British armed forces. The event is free to attend.
